Hello, I'm Jon Disla

A passionate Web Developer from the US.

Working on the web for one year and loving everything about it.

A little about me
The start

I started dabbling in Geocities and Angelfire during the late 90s/2000s. Web development was a hobby for me back then. I still use principles learnt long ago and have the same hunger to learn and desire to innovate.


Fast forward to today, I love web development even more. I really enjoy the design and user experience aspect of the web; from simple animations to complex responsive designs.

What I'm made of

I'm am a mix of a technical and artistic roots. I studied music and have a sound engineering education coupled with programming and analysis training in college. I also served an eclesiastical mission for two years.


My journey through web development has taken me from using the MERN stack to now jumping into Wordpress and Laravel. I've had to learn many different disciplines through out my time and I have a tendency to not stop until a problem is solved.

Value

I hold a technical diploma in web development with focus on programming from Valencia College. Currently, I work at a private school as IT admin. Recently I took over as webmaster of the main school site.


I also work full time at Charter as tech support. I would love to focus more on the design and development side of technology. I love the creative and design aspect of the web and find this career exciting and fulfilling.


Corkboard


Organize your resources and collaborate with your peers. How many favorites do you have in your browser? If you have ever added a favorite in your browser, you would see how un-appealing and messy your resources are stored.


With corkboard your resources are categorized depending on your preferences. It has a sleek design with a picture of the resource. Create as many boards as you want and invite friends to your board, the possibilities are endless.

Park Finder


The goal of Park Finder is to help plan your next trip to Florida national parks. With data gathered from the national parks and google maps APIs we managed to pull off a simple yet informative app. Included is also weather information from an open weather API.


Currently the app only has data for Florida national parks.
